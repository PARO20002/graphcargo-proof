In parallel to commercial operations, we pursue a research grant with Fraunhofer under Horizon Europe Cluster 5 (Climate, Energy and Mobility). Research topic: causal intelligence for supply chain resilience, transferable from air freight to sea freight. Fraunhofer awaits company formation to file the joint proposal. A successful grant would fund 24 months of research including data licences and HPC compute.
